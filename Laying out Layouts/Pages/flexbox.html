How much waste can we store?

The Yucca mountain holds up to 77,000 metric tons of nuclear waste. The United States currently produces about 2,000 metric tons of spent fuel each year. This means the Yucca mountain can hold up to 38 years worth of waste. Additionally, researchers across the world are attempting to find ways to reuse the nuclear waste produced to further produce more clean energy.
